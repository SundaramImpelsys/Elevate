Frequently Asked Questions

What is Elevate?

Elevate is a learning management system (LMS) designed to help organizations deliver engaging and effective online education. It supports curriculum development, certifications, social learning, and more.

 How do I join a course on Elevate?

You can join a course by logging into your Elevate account and navigating to the course you wish to enroll in. Ensure you have a stable internet connection and the necessary software installed.

 What if I miss a live session?

If you miss a live session, recordings may be available in your course materials. Check with your instructor for more details on accessing the recordings.

 Can I ask questions during the session?

Yes! You can ask questions during the session using the chat feature or by unmuting yourself if permitted by the instructor.

 What technical requirements do I need?

You will need a computer or tablet with a reliable internet connection, a webcam, and a microphone. Ensure your software is up to date.
